DHOKLA

This guide will help you make fluffy and soft dhokla

But

a traditonal khaman dhokla needs its companion i.e. dhaniya pudina chutney

INGREDIENTS :

  • gram flour - 1 cup
  • curd - 1/2 cup
  • water - 1/4 cup
  • salt and sugar as per taste
  • baking soda - 1/2 tsp
  • baking powder - 1 tsp

Khaman Dhokla is a sweet and savoury dish originated in gujarat. This amazing dish is usually enjoyed with some green chutney on the side.

STEPS OF PREPRATION:
  1. take a bowl
  2. put the flour
  3. mix the sugar and salt
  4. mix in the curd and then slowly add water
  5. now add the baking powder and baking soda
  6. keep it aside for 5 minutes
COOKING DIRECTIONS:
  1. take a big patila
  2. add water and stand in it
  3. heat this with cover on for 2 minutes
  4. then place the pan with the dhokla batter in it
  5. cook for 15-20 minutes
